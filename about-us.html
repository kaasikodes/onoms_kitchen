About Us

The Yellow Plate is Abuja’s premier 24-hour online food delivery cafe & bistro.

Located in the heart of the city, The Yellow Plate is the place you come because you’re already friends of ours, or you soon will be. We love yummy food, authentic service, great libations and friendly faces. Available 24-hours day, our food delivery service is available to all parts of Abuja.

We’re cooking ingredient-driven food where inspiration comes from our personal experiences or one of our many customers, past and present. Our food is also inspired by local and international cuisines, using ingredients that are seasonal and raised or caught with care.
